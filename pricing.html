Frequently asked questions

What are Tests?

Thoroughly evaluate candidates' actual coding skills and screen in great candidates. Create a challenge to assess the exact skill set that youâ€™re hiring for, invite candidates to solve your challenges, and review scores.

What are Code Reviews?

Code Review questions allow candidates to submit comments on source code submissions just like a standard peer review. These questions provide insight into multiple candidate skills such as their programming, communication, and design expertise.

What are Interviews?

Conduct real-time technical interviews and evaluate candidates' technical skills. Engage with candidates from anywhere through live chat, audio, and video, all in one browser, enabling a fully interactive interview experience.

What is Benchmarking?

Validate candidate skills proficiency by ranking test scores against every relevant result from the entire candidate pool or the entire Steamlance community. Highlight strong signals from your interview panel by ranking each interviewer rating with that same interviewer's ratings history.

What are Projects?

Projects let you assess candidates on role-specific skills through real-world scenarios. Get a better understanding of on-the-job skills for front-end, back-end, full-stack, data science, and devops roles.
